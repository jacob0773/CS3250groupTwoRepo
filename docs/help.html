Features

This is where you can learn to use the features for Quick Notes.

Change Color

When loading Quick Notes, there should be a button label as "Change Color", that will allow you to change the background of each individual note you make.

Bold and Hightlight

To bold or highlight the text that you want, highlight the text with your mouse and click on the wanted button. To undo either option, you have to highlight the text you want to unbold with your mouse, and click on the button you want to undo.

New Note

Clicking on "New Note" will allow you to have a new sticky note allow with your current one.
